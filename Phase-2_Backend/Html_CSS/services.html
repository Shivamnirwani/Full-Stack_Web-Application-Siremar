Services

Benefits for Businesses

We provide Businesses to understand various possible scenarios where they can make profits. They are also given subsidies based on their investment for the Island of Margarita.

Benefits for Travellers

We provide residents of Margarita numerous advantages for their travel needs both inside the Island as well as for travelling out of Margarita. We give discounts on ferry and flight tickets. We also have offers where residents can redeem discounts if they have met certain conditions.

Benefits for School Admission

Siremar cares about the parents who live on Margarita. We help parents in selecting schools for their wards based on the county they reside. We provide them list of schools available, the fee structure as well as the pros and cons of that particular school. This makes the process of selectig the perfect school less cumbersome.
